Your AviOS guide

A simple route through the app. There is no prize for rushing.

1

Start at Dashboard

See what is open, choose one daily focus, and record a mood or quick note. It is a snapshot, not a judgement.

2

Use Tasks for clear actions

Add something you can actually complete. Mark it done when finished, pin what matters now, and archive what no longer needs attention.

3

Use Habits for repetition

Add behaviours you want to repeat, then mark them once each day. Missing a day is information, not failure.

4

Use Expenses for money you track

Record what you need to pay or receive. Choose the currency, category, and exchange rate carefully before saving.

5

Make Settings your own

Add your personal information and picture. API settings are optional and intended for future integrations.

6

Finish small

One completed task and one honest check-in are useful progress. Close AviOS when it has helped enough for today.
