Questions, answered

You do not need to know everything before you begin. AviOS is meant to help, not examine you.

What is AviOS?

AviOS is your personal place for tasks, habits, expenses, and a gentle daily overview. Use the parts that help you today; the rest can wait.

Where is my information saved?

Your information is saved locally in files on this computer. The web pages and terminal application share the same data.

Does AviOS send my information online?

No. The current application works locally. Adding API settings only saves configuration; it does not send anything or activate a service.

What happens when I archive or delete something?

Archived items are put aside but kept. Deleted items can still be restored until you choose to remove them permanently.

What should I do if something looks wrong?

Pause, avoid deleting data, and note what you clicked. Restarting the local server is a sensible first check. Your work is more recoverable when you move calmly.
